About FRIM fish & foods

Frim Fish and Farms, is created to exploit opportunities presented by Blue Economy through Fish Cage Farming, Sale and supply of Fresh organic Farm produce, Trade in Fresh Fish /Fried or Baked Fish Products within and outside Kenyan Market.
As pioneers in the industry, Frim Fish & Foods is proud to lead the way in fish cage farming—a revolutionary method that ensures sustainable and high-quality fish production. Our investment in cutting-edge fish cage farming techniques allows us to harvest fish in their natural habitat while maintaining the highest standards of quality and environmental responsibility.

The business model is inspired by the need to satisfy the local and international market demand for quality products,fresh and authentic foods due to ever rising health and nutritional needs.

  • Invest in Fish Cage Farming.
  • Sale and supply of Fresh organic Farm produce.
  • Trade in Fresh Fish /Fried or Baked Fish Products within and outside Kenyan Market.
